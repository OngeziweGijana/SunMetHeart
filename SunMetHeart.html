About the Book

The Day the Sun Met My Heart

Author: Zimasa Gijana

Genre: Romance / Coming of Age / African Fiction

Language: English with elements of isiXhosa

Length: Approx. 70-100 pages

Formats: eBook & Hardcover

In a small Eastern Cape town, a young woman writes a letter to a love she thought she had lost forever. As memories of whispered promises and bittersweet goodbyes resurface, she must confront the past to reclaim her future.

A story of first love, cultural identity, and the courage to speak one's truth, The Day the Sun Met My Heart is a lyrical journey through the highs and lows of the heart.

About the Author

Zimasa Gijana

Zimasa Gijana is a South African writer and storyteller whose work celebrates love, heritage, and the power of words. Drawing inspiration from her Xhosa roots, she crafts narratives that resonate with authenticity and emotional depth.

When she's not writing, Zimasa enjoys singing as she is a member and the chairperson of Balatedi Ba Morena Church Choir and a member of St. Jacobs Prophetic Church Of Christ.
